# Step 07 — Choose staff

## What you’ll do
Select a preferred staff member (when available), or continue without selecting one.

## Where you are in the app
Booking flow

## Step-by-step
1. Start a booking by picking a service variant from a business page.
2. Look for a staff selection step (a list of team members).
3. If a staff list is available:
   1. Tap the staff member you want.
   2. Continue to time selection.
4. If you don’t see any staff selection UI, continue to the next step (time selection).

## What to expect
- **MVP note (staff selection not in UI)**: The current MVP does not present a staff picker screen.
- Your booking may still include a staff member behind the scenes (or it may remain unassigned).
- In **My Bookings**, you may see a line like “with {staff name}” when staff is assigned.

## Common variations
- **Salon policy**: Some businesses assign staff automatically based on availability and may not allow choosing a specific person.
- **Staff not shown**: If no staff is assigned, the booking list won’t show a “with …” line.

## Troubleshooting
- **You can’t choose a staff member**: This is expected in the MVP. Proceed with time selection and booking.
- **You expected a specific staff member but don’t see it**: Staff selection may not be available for that business or service yet.
