# Step 13 — Favorites

## What you’ll do
Save businesses you like so you can find them quickly later.

## Where you are in the app
**Explore** tab (and business details pages)

## Step-by-step
1. In **Explore**, open a business you like.
2. Look for a “Favorite”, “Save”, or heart icon.
3. If favorites are available, tap it to add the business to your saved list.
4. If you don’t see favorites, use one of the MVP-friendly alternatives:
   - Take note of the business name
   - Take a screenshot of the business page

## What to expect
- **MVP note (favorites not implemented)**: The current MVP does not include a favorites UI or a saved list screen.

## Common variations
- **Platform**: Screenshot shortcuts differ (iOS vs Android).
- **Business discovery**: Without favorites or search, you’ll return to **Explore** and scroll to find businesses again.

## Troubleshooting
- **You can’t find a favorites section**: That’s expected in the MVP. Use the alternatives above until favorites are added.
