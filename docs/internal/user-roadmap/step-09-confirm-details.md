# Step 09 — Confirm details

## What you’ll do
Double-check you’ve chosen the right business, service, and time, then confirm your booking.

## Where you are in the app
Booking flow → **Select Time**

## Step-by-step
1. On **Select Time**, confirm you’re booking the correct appointment:
   - If you picked the wrong business, use back navigation to return to **Explore**.
   - If you picked the wrong service, go back to the business page and select a different service variant.
2. Select your desired time slot.
3. Tap **Book Appointment** to confirm.
4. When you see the success message, tap **OK**.

## What to expect
- **MVP note (no review screen)**: The current MVP does not have a separate “Review & confirm” summary screen.
- Confirmation happens when you tap **Book Appointment**.
- On success you’ll see an alert: **“Appointment booked!”** and then you’ll be taken to **My Bookings**.

## Common variations
- **Fast booking**: If your network is quick, the confirmation may feel instant.
- **Slow booking**: You may see a spinner while the booking request is being submitted.

## Troubleshooting
- **Booking Failed**: Read the message in the alert. Common causes are unavailable slots or connectivity issues.
- **Nothing happens when booking**: Ensure you selected a time and try again; if it persists, restart the app.
