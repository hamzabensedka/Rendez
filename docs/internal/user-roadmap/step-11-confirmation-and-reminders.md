# Step 11 — Confirmation and reminders

## What you’ll do
Confirm your appointment was created successfully and learn where to find it later.

## Where you are in the app
Booking flow → success confirmation → **Bookings** tab (My Bookings)

## Step-by-step
1. After tapping **Book Appointment**, wait for the success message.
2. On the **Success** alert (“Appointment booked!”), tap **OK**.
3. You’ll be taken to the **Bookings** tab automatically.
4. Verify your appointment appears in **My Bookings**.

## What to expect
- The app displays an alert confirming the booking succeeded.
- **My Bookings** lists upcoming bookings with:
  - Business name
  - Staff (only if assigned)
  - Date and time
  - Status

## Common variations
- **MVP note (no reminders)**: The current MVP does not send push notifications, SMS, or email reminders.
- **Time display**: Date/time formatting depends on your device locale settings.

## Troubleshooting
- **You didn’t see the success message**: Go to **Bookings** and check if the appointment exists.
- **Booking succeeded but doesn’t appear**: Pull-to-refresh isn’t available in the current UI; try switching tabs or reopening the app to reload.
