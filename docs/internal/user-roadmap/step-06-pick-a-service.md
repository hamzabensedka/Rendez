# Step 06 — Pick a service

## What you’ll do
Choose a specific service option (service variant) to start the booking flow.

## Where you are in the app
Business details page (opened from **Explore**)

## Step-by-step
1. From **Explore**, tap a business to open its details page.
2. Scroll to the **Services** section.
3. Find the service you want (e.g., “Haircut”) and then pick the exact option under it (a “variant”).
4. Tap the service variant card to continue.

## What to expect
- Each service variant may show:
  - Variant name
  - Duration (e.g., “45 min”)
  - Price (only shown if a price is set)
- Tapping a variant takes you to the booking flow (time selection).

## Common variations
- **Price missing**: If no price is configured, the UI won’t show a euro amount.
- **Service structure**: Businesses may group services differently depending on how their catalog is set up.

## Troubleshooting
- **No services listed**: The business may not have services configured yet; go back and pick another business.
- **Tapping a service does nothing**: Check your connection and retry; if it persists, force-close and reopen the app.
