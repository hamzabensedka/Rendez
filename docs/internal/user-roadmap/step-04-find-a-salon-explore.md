# Step 04 — Find a salon (Explore)

## What you’ll do
Browse the list of available businesses and open a business page to view details and services.

## Where you are in the app
**Explore** tab

## Step-by-step
1. Tap the **Explore** tab.
2. Scroll the list of businesses.
3. On each business card, note any available:
   - **Category** (if provided)
   - **Rating** (shown when there are ratings)
4. Tap a business card to open its details page.

## What to expect
- The Explore screen shows a list of businesses fetched from the backend.
- Tapping a business opens the business details page with:
  - Business name (and category if available)
  - An **About** section (if the business has a description)
  - A **Services** section where you can start booking

## Common variations
- **Empty state**: If there are no businesses, you’ll see **No businesses found**.
- **iOS/Android scrolling**: Momentum scrolling differs slightly, but the content is the same.

## Troubleshooting
- **“No businesses found”**: Try again later, or confirm your environment has data (for local dev, ensure the database was seeded).
- **Businesses don’t load**: Check your internet connection and that the API endpoint configured for the app is reachable.
