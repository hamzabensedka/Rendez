# Step 05 — Filter and compare

## What you’ll do
Narrow down your options and compare businesses so you can pick the best fit.

## Where you are in the app
**Explore** tab (and business details pages)

## Step-by-step
1. In **Explore**, scroll through the business list and shortlist a few options.
2. Tap a business to open its details.
3. Review what’s available on the business page:
   - **Category** (if present)
   - **About** (description, if present)
   - **Services** (what you can book)
4. Use your device’s back navigation to return to **Explore** and open a different business to compare.

## What to expect
- **MVP note (no filters yet)**: The current MVP does not include filter chips, search, sorting, or map view.
- Comparison is done by opening multiple business pages and comparing their service offerings and details.

## Common variations
- **Ratings not shown**: Ratings only appear on the Explore card when `ratingCount > 0`.
- **Business info varies**: Some businesses may not have an “About” description yet.

## Troubleshooting
- **Hard to find a specific business**: In the MVP there is no search. Scroll through the list and use category labels (when present) to narrow down.
- **A business page shows “Business not found”**: The business may have been removed or the data failed to load; go back and try another business.
