# Step 08 — Choose date and time

## What you’ll do
Pick an available appointment time slot for your chosen service.

## Where you are in the app
Booking flow → **Select Time**

## Step-by-step
1. After choosing a service variant, you’ll land on the **Select Time** screen.
2. Review the **Available Times** grid.
3. Tap a time slot to select it.
4. Tap **Book Appointment**.

## What to expect
- Selecting a slot highlights it.
- **Book Appointment** stays disabled until you’ve selected a slot.
- If there are no available times, you’ll see **No available slots**.

## Common variations
- **MVP note (date selection)**: The current MVP requests availability for “today” and does not include a date picker yet.
- **Time formatting**: Times may format slightly differently depending on device locale settings (12-hour vs 24-hour).

## Troubleshooting
- **No available slots**: Try a different service or business, or check back later.
- **The app says “Please select a time slot”**: Tap an available time first, then retry **Book Appointment**.
