# Step 03 — Profile basics

## What you’ll do
Open your profile to confirm your account details and learn where to sign out.

## Where you are in the app
**Profile** tab

## Step-by-step
1. Tap the **Profile** tab in the bottom navigation.
2. Review the information shown in your profile card:
   - **Name**
   - **Email**
   - **Role**
3. If you’re using a shared device, confirm you know how to end your session:
   1. Tap **Sign Out**.
   2. Confirm you are returned to the sign-in screen.
4. Sign back in if you want to continue using the app.

## What to expect
- The Profile screen shows your currently signed-in user data.
- Tapping **Sign Out** returns you to the login screen.

## Common variations
- **MVP note (read-only profile)**: In the current MVP, Profile is view-only (there’s no “Edit Profile” flow yet).
- **iOS/Android navigation**: Both platforms use the same tab-based navigation; the main difference is how you “go back” (gesture vs back button).

## Troubleshooting
- **Profile fields are blank**: Sign out and sign in again to refresh your session.
- **You’re redirected to login unexpectedly**: Your session may have expired; sign in again.
- **Sign out doesn’t seem to work**: Force-close the app and reopen; you should be asked to sign in again.
