# Step 14 — After the appointment

## What you’ll do
Check your booking status and understand what post-appointment actions are available in the MVP.

## Where you are in the app
**Bookings** tab and **Profile** tab

## Step-by-step
1. Open the **Bookings** tab and review your appointment entry.
2. After your appointment time has passed, check whether the booking status changes.
3. If you want to sign out (for example, on a shared device), go to **Profile** → **Sign Out**.

## What to expect
- **MVP note (limited post-appointment features)**: The current MVP does not include:
  - Reviews/ratings submission
  - Rebooking shortcuts
  - Receipts/invoices
  - Push/email reminders or follow-ups

## Common variations
- **Status handling**: Status text depends on how the backend updates appointments over time.
- **Staff display**: Some bookings may show staff; others may not.

## Troubleshooting
- **You expected to leave a review**: Reviews are not available in the MVP yet.
- **Your booking status doesn’t update**: Reload the app later; if the backend doesn’t update statuses, it may remain unchanged in the list.
