# Step 10 — Payment or deposit

## What you’ll do
Understand how payment works for your booking, and what to do if the app doesn’t collect payment yet.

## Where you are in the app
Booking flow / post-booking confirmation

## Step-by-step
1. Complete your booking (Step 09).
2. Look for any payment or deposit step during checkout.
3. If no payment screen appears, proceed to your booking confirmation and check **My Bookings**.

## What to expect
- **MVP note (no payments in-app)**: The current MVP does not include an in-app payment or deposit collection step.
- Payment (if required) is typically handled by the business outside the app (e.g., at the venue).

## Common variations
- **Business policy differences**: Some businesses may require a deposit, while others allow pay-at-appointment.
- **Price visibility**: Some service variants may not display a price in the MVP if price is not configured.

## Troubleshooting
- **You expected to pay in the app**: This is not available in the MVP. Check the business’s policies outside the app.
- **No price is shown**: The service may not have a price configured yet; confirm with the business if needed.
