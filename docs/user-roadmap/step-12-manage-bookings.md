# Step 12 — Manage bookings

## What you’ll do
View your upcoming appointments and understand what changes you can (and can’t) make in the MVP.

## Where you are in the app
**Bookings** tab → **My Bookings**

## Step-by-step
1. Tap the **Bookings** tab.
2. Review your upcoming bookings in the list.
3. For each booking, check:
   - Business name
   - Date/time
   - Staff (if assigned)
   - Status

## What to expect
- If you have upcoming bookings, each one appears as a card in **My Bookings**.
- If you don’t have any, you’ll see **No upcoming bookings**.

## Common variations
- **MVP note (limited management)**: The current MVP provides a read-only list. There is no in-app cancel/reschedule flow yet.
- **MVP note (refresh)**: There is no pull-to-refresh; reopen the tab or app to see new bookings.
- **Staff shown or not**: Some bookings may show “with …”; others may have no staff listed.

## Troubleshooting
- **Your new booking isn’t visible**: Reopen the app or switch tabs and come back to trigger a reload.
- **You need to change/cancel**: In the MVP, contact the business directly (outside the app).
