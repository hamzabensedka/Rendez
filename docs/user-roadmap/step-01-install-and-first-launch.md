# Step 01 — Install and first launch

## What you’ll do
Install the app and open it for the first time so you can start exploring and (optionally) book an appointment.

## Where you are in the app
App launch → authentication screens (if prompted)

## Step-by-step
1. Install the app from the distribution method you were given (App Store / Google Play / internal build).
2. Open the app.
3. On first launch, watch for any sign-in prompt:
   - If you land on **Welcome Back** (sign in screen), go to Step 02.
   - If you land directly in the app, open **Explore** to start browsing.
4. If your device asks for permissions (for example, notifications), choose **Allow** or **Don’t Allow** based on your preference.
5. Confirm you can see the bottom navigation tabs (when available): **Explore**, **Bookings**, **Profile**.

## What to expect
- Some builds may require you to sign in before showing the main tabs. Other builds may allow browsing first and only require sign-in when you try to book.
- After you’re signed in, you can access the bottom tabs: **Explore**, **Bookings**, and **Profile**.

## Common variations
- **iOS**: You may see a system prompt for notifications; you can change this later in iOS Settings.
- **Android**: Back navigation typically uses the system back gesture/button.
- **First-time navigation**: Depending on the build, you may enter the app immediately or be routed to authentication first.

## Troubleshooting
- **The app shows a blank screen on launch**: Force-close and reopen. If it persists, reinstall the app.
- **You can’t get past the sign-in screen**: Check your internet connection and proceed to Step 02 to create an account or use valid credentials.
- **Explore shows no content**: Confirm you have connectivity and try again later.
