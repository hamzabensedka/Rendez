# Progress Report for Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone project aims to develop a mobile application that connects users with local businesses, allowing them to discover, book, and manage appointments. This report provides an overview of the project's progress, highlighting completed features, pending tasks, and next priorities.
## Completed Features
The following features have been fully implemented and meet the acceptance criteria outlined in the product specification:
* User Authentication: Users can create an account, log in, and reset their password.
* Business Search & Discovery: Users can search for businesses by name, category, or location.
* Business Detail View: Users can view detailed information about a business, including hours, services, and reviews.
* Booking Flow: Users can book appointments with businesses, selecting a service and time slot, and confirming their booking.
* Appointment Management: Users can view their upcoming appointments and cancel or reschedule them.
* Availability & Slot Computation: The app computes available time slots for businesses and handles multiple bookings per time slot.
* Payment Integration: The app integrates with a payment gateway, processing payments and handling payment errors.
## Pending Features
The following features are partially implemented or still pending:
* Guest Browse & Explore: Guests can view business listings, but search functionality is still pending.
* Map-based Search: Users can view businesses on a map, but filtering by category is still pending.
* Service Categories: Businesses can be filtered by service category, but users cannot yet view services offered by a business.
* Favorites: Users can add businesses to their favorites, but viewing favorite businesses is still pending.
* User Profile: Users can view their profile information, but editing profile information is still pending.
* Reviews & Ratings: Users can leave reviews and ratings, but businesses cannot yet respond to reviews.
* Provider / Business Owner Portal: Businesses can view their bookings, but managing services and hours is still pending.
* Admin Dashboard: Admins can view app data and analytics, but managing app settings is still pending.
* Background Jobs (BullMQ): The app can run background jobs, but handling job failures is still pending.
## Next Priorities
Based on the product specification and current progress, the next priorities are:
* Completing the Guest Browse & Explore feature, including search functionality.
* Implementing filtering by category on the map view.
* Enabling users to view services offered by a business.
* Completing the Favorites feature, allowing users to view their favorite businesses.
* Implementing editing of profile information for users.
* Enabling businesses to respond to reviews.
* Completing the Provider / Business Owner Portal, allowing businesses to manage their services and hours.
* Implementing managing of app settings for admins.
* Completing the Background Jobs (BullMQ) feature, handling job failures.
## Completion Percentage
Based on the completed and pending features, the project is approximately 70% complete.
## Conclusion
The Planity Clone project has made significant progress, with several key features fully implemented. However, there are still pending tasks that need to be completed to meet the product specification. The next priorities have been identified, and the team will focus on completing these tasks to deliver a high-quality application.
