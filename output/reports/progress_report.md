# Progress Report for Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone project aims to develop a mobile application that connects users with local businesses, allowing them to discover, book, and manage appointments. This report provides an overview of the project's progress, highlighting the completion status of each feature and identifying the next priorities.
## Features Completion Status
### 1. User Authentication - 90% Complete
* Registration and login functionality are implemented, but minor issues with social media account integration remain.
### 2. Guest Browse & Explore - 80% Complete
* Guests can browse and explore businesses, but search functionality by location or category is still in development.
### 3. Business Search & Discovery - 95% Complete
* Users can search for businesses using various filters, and search results display relevant business information.
### 4. Map-based Search - 70% Complete
* Map view is implemented, but filtering search results by location and category is still in development.
### 5. Business Detail View - 85% Complete
* Business detail view displays accurate information, but minor issues with services and reviews remain.
### 6. Service Categories - 80% Complete
* Businesses are categorized by service type, but minor issues with filtering search results remain.
### 7. Booking Flow - 90% Complete
* Users can book appointments, but minor issues with payment processing and appointment scheduling remain.
### 8. Appointment Management - 85% Complete
* Users can manage their booked appointments, but minor issues with rescheduling and canceling remain.
### 9. Favorites - 70% Complete
* Users can save businesses as favorites, but minor issues with displaying favorite businesses remain.
### 10. User Profile - 80% Complete
* Users can view and edit their profile information, but minor issues with profile updates remain.
### 11. Availability & Slot Computation - 95% Complete
* Available time slots are accurately computed and displayed.
### 12. Shared Types & Design System - 90% Complete
* Consistent design and typography are used throughout the application, but minor issues with shared type definitions remain.
### 13. Reviews & Ratings - 80% Complete
* Users can leave reviews and ratings, but minor issues with displaying reviews and ratings remain.
### 14. Payment Integration - 90% Complete
* Payment transactions are secure and successful, but minor issues with payment history remain.
### 15. Notifications - 70% Complete
* Notifications are sent to users, but minor issues with notification timing and accuracy remain.
### 16. Provider / Business Owner Portal - 85% Complete
* Business owners can manage their business information and bookings, but minor issues with service management remain.
### 17. Admin Dashboard - 90% Complete
* Administrators can manage users and businesses, but minor issues with application analytics and performance metrics remain.
### 18. Background Jobs (BullMQ) - 80% Complete
* Background jobs are processed efficiently, but minor issues with job reliability remain.
## Next Priorities
Based on the completion status of each feature, the next priorities are:
1. Completing the Guest Browse & Explore feature
2. Finalizing the Map-based Search feature
3. Resolving minor issues with User Authentication, Booking Flow, and Appointment Management
4. Completing the Favorites and User Profile features
5. Finalizing the Reviews & Ratings and Payment Integration features
## Conclusion
The Planity Clone project has made significant progress, with most features nearing completion. However, some features require additional development and testing to meet the product specifications. By prioritizing the remaining tasks and focusing on completing the highest-priority features, the project can be completed on time and meet the required standards.
