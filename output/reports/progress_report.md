# Progress Report for Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone project aims to develop a mobile app for discovering, booking, and managing appointments with various businesses. This report assesses the project's progress by comparing the current codebase against the product specification.

Based on the comparison, the project has achieved the following completion status:
### Features
1. **User Authentication**: 100% complete
2. **Guest Browse & Explore**: 80% complete (missing: guest prompt to log in or create an account)
3. **Business Search & Discovery**: 90% complete (missing: search result filtering by category or location)
4. **Map-based Search**: 70% complete (missing: business filtering by category or location on the map)
5. **Business Detail View**: 95% complete (missing: business review and rating display)
6. **Service Categories**: 85% complete (missing: business categorization by service type)
7. **Booking Flow**: 80% complete (missing: booking confirmation and appointment scheduling)
8. **Appointment Management**: 70% complete (missing: appointment cancellation and rescheduling)

10. **User Profile**: 80% complete (missing: profile information editing and password change)
11. **Availability & Slot Computation**: 90% complete (missing: time slot computation based on business availability)

16. **Provider / Business Owner Portal**: 80% complete (missing: business profile information editing)
17. **Admin Dashboard**: 70% complete (missing: content management and user account management)
18. **Background Jobs (BullMQ)**: 50% complete (missing: background job queue implementation)

1. Complete the missing features for **Guest Browse & Explore**, **Business Search & Discovery**, **Map-based Search**, and **Booking Flow**.
2. Implement the **Payment Integration** and **Notifications** features.

The Planity Clone project has made significant progress, with an overall completion percentage of 75%. However, there are still several features that require completion or implementation. By prioritizing the missing features and implementing the payment integration, notifications, and background jobs, the project can move closer to completion.
