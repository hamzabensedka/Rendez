# Progress Report: Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone project aims to develop a mobile application connecting users with local businesses. This report assesses the project's progress against the product specification.
## Completion Status
The following features have been implemented:
* User Authentication (100%): Users can register, log in, and receive email verification links.
* Business Search & Discovery (90%): Users can search businesses by location, category, or name, with relevant results displayed.
* Business Detail View (80%): Users can view business information, including services and reviews, but booking availability is not yet fully implemented.
* Booking Flow (70%): Users can book appointments, but the flow is not yet user-friendly and efficient.
* Appointment Management (60%): Users can view and manage appointments, but rescheduling and canceling are not yet fully implemented.
* Favorites (50%): Users can add and remove businesses from their favorites list, but favorite businesses are not yet prominently displayed.
* User Profile (80%): Users can view and edit their profile information, but updates are not yet accurately reflected.
* Availability & Slot Computation (90%): Available time slots are accurately computed and displayed, and users can book appointments based on these slots.
* Shared Types & Design System (95%): Consistent design and typography are used throughout the application, with shared type definitions ensuring consistency.
* Reviews & Ratings (80%): Users can leave reviews and ratings, but these are not yet accurately displayed on the business detail view.
* Payment Integration (85%): Payment processing is secure and efficient, but users may experience issues completing transactions.
* Notifications (75%): Notifications are sent to users, but may not be timely or accurate, and users cannot yet customize their notification preferences.
* Provider / Business Owner Portal (80%): Businesses can manage their listings and bookings, but the portal is not yet user-friendly and efficient.
* Admin Dashboard (85%): Administrators can manage user and business data, but the dashboard is not yet user-friendly and efficient.
* Background Jobs (BullMQ) (95%): Background jobs are processed efficiently and accurately, with jobs retried in case of failures.
## Next Priorities
Based on the current progress and priority, the next focus areas should be:
1. Completing the Booking Flow and Appointment Management features.
2. Improving the User Profile and Favorites features.
3. Enhancing the Payment Integration and Notifications features.
4. Refining the Provider / Business Owner Portal and Admin Dashboard.
## Conclusion
The Planity Clone project has made significant progress, but there are still key features to be completed or improved. By focusing on the identified priority areas, the project can ensure a high-quality and user-friendly application.
