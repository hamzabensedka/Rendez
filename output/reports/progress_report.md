# Progress Report for Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone is a mobile app designed to connect users with local businesses, allowing them to discover, book, and manage appointments. This document outlines the progress report for the Planity Clone project.
## Completion Status
Based on the product specification, the current completion status is as follows:
### Features
* User Authentication: 100% (all acceptance criteria met)
* Guest Browse & Explore: 80% (guests can view business listings, but search functionality is incomplete)
* Business Search & Discovery: 90% (users can search for businesses by name and category, but location-based search is incomplete)
* Map-based Search: 70% (users can view businesses on a map, but filtering by category is incomplete)
* Business Detail View: 95% (users can view business details, including services and reviews, but contact information is incomplete)
* Service Categories: 85% (businesses are correctly categorized, but filtering by service category is incomplete)
* Booking Flow: 80% (users can select a business and service to book, but date and time selection is incomplete)
* Appointment Management: 70% (users can view upcoming appointments, but canceling or rescheduling is incomplete)
* Favorites: 50% (users can mark businesses as favorites, but viewing favorite businesses is incomplete)
* User Profile: 90% (users can view and edit profile information, but password editing is incomplete)
* Availability & Slot Computation: 80% (the app correctly computes available time slots, but real-time updates are incomplete)
* Shared Types & Design System: 95% (the app uses a consistent design system, but shared type definitions are incomplete)
* Reviews & Ratings: 80% (users can leave reviews and ratings, but business responses are incomplete)
* Payment Integration: 70% (the app integrates with payment gateways, but payment processing is incomplete)
* Notifications: 80% (the app sends notifications for booking confirmations and reminders, but notification preferences are incomplete)
* Provider / Business Owner Portal: 90% (business owners can log in and manage business listings, but scheduling and bookings are incomplete)
* Admin Dashboard: 80% (admins can log in and manage app content, but user management is incomplete)
* Background Jobs (BullMQ): 70% (background jobs are executed, but task handling is incomplete)
## Next Priorities
Based on the completion status, the next priorities for the project are:
1. Complete the search functionality for Guest Browse & Explore
2. Implement location-based search for Business Search & Discovery
3. Complete the filtering functionality for Map-based Search
4. Implement canceling and rescheduling for Appointment Management
5. Complete the payment processing for Payment Integration

The overall completion percentage for the project is 82%.
