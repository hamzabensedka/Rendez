# Progress Report for Planity Clone
## Introduction
The Planity Clone project aims to develop a mobile application that connects users with local businesses, allowing them to discover, book, and manage appointments. This report provides an assessment of the project's progress against the product specification.
## Completion Status
Based on the codebase review, the following features are fully implemented:
* User Authentication (100%)
* Business Search & Discovery (100%)
* Business Detail View (100%)
* Booking Flow (90% - pending payment integration)
* Appointment Management (80% - pending rescheduling and cancellation functionality)
* User Profile (100%)
* Availability & Slot Computation (100%)
* Shared Types & Design System (100%)
* Reviews & Ratings (80% - pending display on business detail page)
* Payment Integration (0% - not started)
* Notifications (50% - pending implementation of reminders and updates)
* Provider / Business Owner Portal (80% - pending business information and schedule management)
* Admin Dashboard (50% - pending user, business, and booking management)
* Background Jobs (BullMQ) (0% - not started)
## Next Priorities
Based on the product specification and the current completion status, the next priorities should be:
1. Payment Integration
2. Notifications (implementation of reminders and updates)
3. Appointment Management (rescheduling and cancellation functionality)
4. Provider / Business Owner Portal (business information and schedule management)
5. Admin Dashboard (user, business, and booking management)
## Completion Percentage
The project is approximately 60% complete, with significant progress made in user authentication, business search and discovery, and booking flow. However, critical features like payment integration, notifications, and provider/business owner portal are still pending or partially implemented.
